Saffron

Saffron is the most expensive plant found in the world. Being so expensive, it is also called red gold. Cultivation of saffron is very easy and simple. Saffron crop does not require much hard work. Tomato

The total duration of Tomato commercial cultivation is about 110 to 140 days. However, yielding starts after 50 to 60 days of sowing. The picking is done for every 10 to 15 days interval from the date of 1st picking. Likewise, a farmer can go for picking for about five times until the last harvest. The farmer can get a total yield of 8 to 12 tonnes/ acre. .
